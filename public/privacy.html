GymSquad Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

GymSquad ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: Your name and email address when you sign up.
  • Workout data: Your weekly training split, daily check-ins, and streak history.
  • Weight data: Any weight entries and goals you choose to log.
  • Friends: Email addresses you use to add friends to your squad.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the GymSquad app.
  • To show your workout activity to friends you have added to your squad.
  • To maintain your streak and progress history.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Your workout data is visible to friends you have added to your squad within the app. We use Firebase (Google) to store and authenticate your data securely.

Data Retention

Your data is stored as long as your account is active. You can stop using the app at any time.

Children's Privacy

GymSquad is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
